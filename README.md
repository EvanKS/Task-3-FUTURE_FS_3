# ☕ The Roasted Bean – Bangalore

*A Real Local Business Website Project*

---

## Project Overview

The Roasted Bean is an artisan coffee café based in Bangalore, India. This project was developed as part of a real-world full stack web development task to build a professional website for a local business.

The objective was to create a **fully functional, responsive, and customer-focused website** that helps the business establish a strong online presence, attract customers, and improve accessibility.

---

##  Objective

* Build a professional website for a local café
* Improve online visibility and credibility
* Provide easy access to menu, contact, and location
* Enhance customer engagement and conversion

---

##  Problem Statement

Many local cafés lack a proper online presence, which leads to:

* Difficulty for customers to discover the business
* No easy way to view menu or offerings
* Limited trust among new customers
* Lack of quick contact or navigation options

---

##  Solution

A modern, responsive website was developed to:

* Showcase menu and offerings clearly
* Enable instant communication via WhatsApp and call
* Provide accurate location using Google Maps
* Build trust through design, content, and testimonials

---

##  Features

*  **Responsive Design** – Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
*  **Menu Section** – Clear display of food & beverages
*  **Google Maps Integration** – Easy location access
*  **Contact Options** – Call, Email, WhatsApp
*  **Gallery Section** – Visual representation of café
*  **Testimonials** – Builds customer trust
*  **Fast & Lightweight** – Optimized performance
