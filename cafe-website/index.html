Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional coffee, The Roasted Bean started as a small cart in 2015. Today, we are Bangalore's premier destination for artisan coffee lovers.

We believe in sustainable practices, direct-trade sourcing, and the community that a great cup of coffee can build. Every cup is brewed with precision and care.
